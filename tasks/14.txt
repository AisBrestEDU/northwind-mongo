Create a SQL query to get Product list of stock is less than the quantity on order:

| ProductName | UnitsInOrder| UnitsInStock |

db.products.aggregate([
    {$project: {_id: 0, ProductName: 1, UnitsOnOrder: 1, UnitsInStock: 1, check: {$lt: ["$UnitsInStock", "$UnitsOnOrder"]}}},
    {$match: {check : true}},
    {$project: {ProductName: 1, UnitsInOrder: "$UnitsOnOrder", UnitsInStock: "$UnitsInStock"}}
]);
